• La La Land

    A musical about a jazz pianist and an aspiring actress trying to make it in Los Angeles. A film about love, dreams and sacrifice. Gosling performed many musical numbers.

  • Drive

    Gosling plays a silent stuntman and auto mechanic who works as a driver for criminals at night. A stylish and tense neo-noir with excellent music.

  • Barbie

    A comedy fantasy in which Gosling played Ken, a charismatic but slightly lost hero in the bright and crazy world of Barbie. An unusual role with elements of irony.

  • The nice gays

    A comedy action film about a private detective and a mercenary investigating a strange case in Los Angeles in the 70s. A light, funny film with a great couple - Gosling and Russell Crowe.

  • The Notebook

    A romantic drama about love between a poor guy and a rich girl who are separated by circumstances, but united by true love. One of the most touching films about feelings.

  • Blade Runner 2049

    Sci-fi and sequel to the cult film of 1982. Gosling plays Officer K, a replicant looking for answers about his origin. A stylish, deep film with a philosophical meaning.
